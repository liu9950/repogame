Gameplay Basics

In REPO Game, you play as a surgical repo agent working for GeneCo. Your job is to locate debtors who have fallen behind on payments for their artificial organs and repossess these organs - by any means necessary.

Core Objectives

  • Identify and locate the target debtor
  • Infiltrate their residence or workplace
  • Subdue or neutralize the target
  • Successfully extract the organ(s)
  • Escape without being caught

Moral System

  • Each target mission offers moral choices
  • Ruthless approach: maximize profit, faster promotions
  • Compassionate approach: seek alternatives for debtors
  • Choices affect your reputation, equipment access, and story endings
